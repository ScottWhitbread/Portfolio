3D FEA Analysis and Optimization of Hinge

In this project, I conducted a comprehensive Finite Element Analysis (FEA) and optimization study on a hinge to improve its performance, durability, and weight efficiency using CATIA V5. Using advanced simulation techniques, I aimed to enhance the design to provide better structural integrity while reducing material usage. Through iterative simulations and mesh refinement, I optimized the hinge geometry by modifying parameters such as thickness, fillet radii, and material distribution.

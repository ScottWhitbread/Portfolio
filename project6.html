Head Cap with Inlet and Outlet Ports

In this project, I created a head cap with Inlet and outlet ports using CATIA V5. The model incorporates various complex surfaces and features, beginning with the use of Revolved Profiles to create the dome-shaped cap. By revolving a 2D sketch around an axis, the symmetrical and smooth geometry of the cap was efficiently generated. The cylindrical inlet and outlet pipes were created using a combination of Pad and Rib operations, enabling the precise creation of intersecting geometries. The base of the head cap was created using Pad and Circular Pattern operations.

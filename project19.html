Drawing of Drone 4

In this sheet, I created a detailed documentation of the propeller assembly for the drone, featuring several views: top, front, and a sectional view labeled "Section C-C." The section view, scaled at 2.5:1, reveals the internal components and their alignment within the propeller assembly, highlighting the positioning of the collar, spacer, and propeller nose. The sectional detail allows for a clear understanding of each part’s interaction and fit within the assembly, especially useful for verifying tolerances and ensuring correct assembly. An exploded view presents the propeller assembly in a disassembled state, labeling each part and aligning it with the bill of materials (BOM) for easy identification. The BOM provides essential details, listing each component’s part number, description, material, and quantity, ensuring streamlined assembly and inventory management.

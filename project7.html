Car Kinematic Model with Cover

In this project, I created a kinematic model for a basic car system and designed a cover for the moving parts, using CATIA V5's DMU Kinematics and Part Design workbenches. The model simulates the general movement of car components and performs a swept volume analysis (transparent yellow highlight) to ensure optimal clearance and functionality of the vehicle's moving parts. The kinematic model was built by defining mechanical joints to replicate the actual motion of the car's components. A cover was then designed to encapsulate the moving components and protect them from external elements.

Created by Scott Whitbread with help from Herick Santos.

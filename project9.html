Advanced Landing Gear Kinematic Model

In this project, I developed a kinematic model of an advanced landing gear system using CATIA V5â€™s DMU Kinematics workbench. This system incorporated complex mechanical joints including universal joints (U-joints) and spherical joints. Additionally, laws to simulate the precise deployment and retraction of the landing gear were created to avoid collision of moving parts.

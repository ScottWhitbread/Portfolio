Drawing of Drone 3

In this sheet, I created a detailed assembly of a single propeller arm of the hexacopter drone. The drawing includes multiple views — top, front, and side — to clearly show the geometry and dimensions of the propeller arm, motor assembly, and propeller. Additionally, the exploded view breaks down each component for easy identification of the part relationships and connection points. A close-up detail, marked as “Detail B,” highlights the motor and propeller connection with precise measurements, ensuring accurate assembly and alignment. This sheet is supported by a detailed bill of materials (BOM) that lists each part number, description, material, and quantity, simplifying the assembly process.

Created by Scott Whitbread with help from Herick Santos.

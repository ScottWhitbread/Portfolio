3D FEA Analysis of T Bracket

In this project, I conducted a 3D Finite Element Analysis (FEA) on a T-bracket using Ansys Mechanical to evaluate its structural performance under various loading conditions and study the solution convergence between hexahedral and tetrahedral elements. This project enhanced my skills in conducting 3D FEA, including setting up appropriate constraints, accurately modeling complex geometries and when to use hexahedral or tetrahedral elements.

Drawing of Drone 2

In this sheet, I created an exploded view drawing of the hexacopter drone assembly using SolidWorks, highlighting the relationship and connectivity between the various components. This drawing includes an itemized bill of materials (BOM), which specifies each part number, quantity, description, and material, ensuring clear documentation for assembly. The exploded view visually details how the components, including the top and bottom boards, arms, and fixture stand, come together, with fasteners shown in their respective positions to aid assembly instructions.
